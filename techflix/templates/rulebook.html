RULEBOOK
  1. This is a Netflix Choices inspired event.
  2. The event unrolls in the form of a story.
  3. The story branches out into different paths, the path to be taken depends on the choices made by the player.
  4. Each choice is preceded by a question, which must be answered in order to continue.
  5. The story must not be looked over, as the choices made will affect the type and weightage of the questions that will show up next.
  6. Successful endings will beget reward points, while reaching a dead end will cause a penalty on your score.
  7. Once an ending is reached, your score is final and you cannot go start over.
  8. Your result will be mailed to the id you register with, kindly ensure you fill in a correct email address.
  9. Questions can either be of CP or CTF type.
  10. Event starts on 29th May at 7pm.
